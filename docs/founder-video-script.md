# Founder video script — 60-second note

**Working title:** Why startups hit a wall after product–market fit — and how I fix it.
**Length:** 45–60 seconds. Native mobile aspect (9:16) for social, cropped to 16:9 for the site.
**Goal:** A founder lands on the site, plays the video, and feels "this person actually gets my problem." Not a pitch. A problem statement they recognise.

---

## Script (approx. 140 words, 50 seconds at natural pace)

> Most post-PMF startups don't have a growth problem. They have a *system* problem.
>
> The product works. The team is busy. Ads are running. Content is shipping. And yet the pipeline still depends on the founder's energy — not a repeatable engine.
>
> I see this in almost every audit I run. Three signals usually give it away: growth decisions still sit with the founder. Reporting tells you what happened but not what to *do*. And paid spend burns without compounding into better decisions next month.
>
> The fix isn't another campaign. It's sequencing: positioning, offer, ICP, then channels. Reporting that forces decisions. And a weekly rhythm the team can run without you in the room.
>
> If that sounds like your startup right now, book a 20-minute Growth Audit. It's free, and you'll leave knowing the real constraint.

---

## Shot notes

- **0:00–0:03** — On-camera, warm lighting, relaxed. Open with the line "Most post-PMF startups don't have a growth problem." Let it land.
- **0:03–0:12** — B-roll cuts: founder looking at Slack / Notion / a dashboard at 11pm. Screen-recordings of messy GA, a calendar packed with ad reviews. Keep text-on-screen to one phrase at a time.
- **0:12–0:35** — Back on camera for the "three signals" beat. Consider a tight lower-third animation listing the three as they're spoken.
- **0:35–0:50** — On camera for "the fix isn't another campaign" — slow down, this is the line the viewer will remember.
- **0:50–0:60** — CTA frame: name, role, URL, and "Book a 20-minute Growth Audit" with the Calendly link.

## Wardrobe / setting

Home office or a neutral studio corner. No agency-style suit-and-whiteboard. This is founder-to-founder.

## Once shot

- Export 16:9 at 1080p for the homepage embed.
- Upload to Wistia (preferred — good thumbnails, closed captions, auto-transcription for GEO) or YouTube unlisted.
- Paste the embed URL into `src/site.ts` → `siteConfig.videoUrl`.
- A 9:16 vertical cut for LinkedIn and X goes in a separate post.

## Transcript (for SEO + GEO)

Paste the transcript into the page via a `<details>` block titled "Read the transcript" — this gives both accessibility and a rich keyword-bearing block for search and LLMs.
