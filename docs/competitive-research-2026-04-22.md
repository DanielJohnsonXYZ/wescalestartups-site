# Competitive Research Notes

Date: 2026-04-22

## Benchmarks Reviewed

- Kalungi: fractional CMO for B2B SaaS, clear service modes, "what you get" sections, agency comparison, and proof-led customer stories.
- SaaS fractional CMO competitors: repeated booking CTAs, results grids, and case-study led proof.
- Xero UK and Tide UK: plain-language SME pages with clear paths by problem, product, and industry.
- Enterprise Nation: practical UK business resource tone and action-oriented guide content.

## Borrowed Patterns

- Make resources useful before a call, not just a newsletter tease.
- Show founder/company credibility without overloading the hero.
- Structure case studies around before / work done / result.
- Add a press/company kit to make third-party references easier.
- Link service and industry pages back to a diagnostic resource.
